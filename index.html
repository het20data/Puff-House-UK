Our Story

A Family Passion
Baked into Every Bite

Puff House is a proud sole trader bakery nestled at 170 High Street, Hounslow. We started with one simple dream — to bring the warmth of authentic Indian baking to our local community, without ever compromising on quality or flavour.

Every item is crafted by hand, using recipes passed down through generations, combined with the finest locally sourced British ingredients. No shortcuts. No compromises. Just honest, delicious baking, seven days a week.
